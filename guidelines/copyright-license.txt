==== Contributing original writing

When you contribute your original writing to this project, you agree that it
will be licensed under the CC BY-SA 2.0 license. You should add your name to the
attributions.txt file in the guide that you are contributing to, so that your
copyright is acknowledged. You may also add the name of an organization or
company that sponsored your work. You may also turn your name into a link to
your Drupal.org user profile, and your organization/company name into a link to
their web site (if you are self-sponsored, you may include a link to your own
web site).

==== Adapting and including existing work

You may include (or adapt) work from the
https://www.drupal.org/documentation[Drupal.org online documentation]
and other works licensed under CC BY-SA 2.0 and compatible licenses in this
project. If you do so, you must add a statement to the attributions.txt file in
the guide that includes:

* The title of the material.
* A link to the material, or if it is print material, publication information.
* Copyright notice (dates, creator, attribution parties).
* An indication of whether you modified the material.

Here is a sample:

----
Adapted from
https://www.drupal.org/node/14307["Documentation copyright and licensing"],
copyright 2000-2015 by the individual contributors to the
https://www.drupal.org/documentation[Drupal Community Documentation]
----
