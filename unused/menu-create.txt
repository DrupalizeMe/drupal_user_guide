=== Creating a Menu

(((Menu,creating)))

==== Goal

If you want additional navigation on your website (for example a navigation
bar in the footer) you can create a new menu.

==== Steps

. Click "Menus" on the "Structure" page (admin/structure). You will see the
"Menu administration" page showing the list of existing menus in your website.

. Click "Add menu".

. In the "Title" field, enter a title that will be shown in the Menu
  administration page.

. In the "Administrative summary" field, enter a description that will be shown
 in the Menu administration page.

. Click "Save".
