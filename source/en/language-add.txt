=== Adding a Language

(((Language,adding)))

==== Goal

Add one or more languages to your site and define which one is used by default.

==== Prerequisite knowledge

<<language-concept>>

==== Site prerequisites

The multilingual modules must be installed. See <<language-enable>>.

==== Steps

. In the _Manage_ administrative menu, navigate to Configuration >
Regional and Language (_admin/config/regional/language_).

. Click _Add language_

. Select your prefered language and click _Add language_.

// Screen shot after adding Spanish from admin/config/regional/language
image:images/language-add-list.png["List of available languages in the
website",width="100%"]

==== Follow-on tasks

* Enable users to switch between languages, by placing the _Language switcher_
block in the sidebar. See <<block-place>>.

* Enable content translation. See <<language-content-config>>.
