==== Steps
There are several options for installing Drupal. They include:

===== Free online options
Using an online provider to get a demo installation of Drupal in 20 minutes or less. https://www.drupal.org/try-drupal[Try Drupal Online].

===== Installing at your hosting provider
If you choose to install Drupal at your hosting provider, your hosting provider
may have specific documentation and/or a one-click install that you can use.

https://www.drupal.org/supporters/hosting[Recommended Drupal hosting providers].


If not, please refer to Installing Drupal on your own:

===== Installing Drupal on your own.
You can install Drupal on your own computer or server via Drush, Drupal's
command-line tool or manually.

====== Installing via Drush
The Drush syntax to install Drupal is:

----
drush dl drupal --drupal-project-rename=example
cd example
drush site-install standard --db-url='mysql://DB_USER:DB_PASS@localhost/DB_NAME' --site-name=Example
----

For additional information refer to http://www.drush.org/en/master[Drush's
documentation].

====== Installing Drupal Manually
To install Drupal manually, you will need to:

. Create a database.
. Get a full-access database user.
. Download Drupal core or the Drupal distribution you would like to use.
. Place the files in the web root.
. Make sites/default writeable.
. Make sites/default/settings.php and sites/default/services.yml and make sure
  these are writeable.
