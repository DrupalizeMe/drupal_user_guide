==== Goal

Keep your site safe and up-to-date by keeping up with the latest security
updates.

==== Steps

There are a few different ways to receive notifications of security releases:

* In the _Manage_ administrative menu, navigate to
_Reports_ > _Available updates_ > Update Manager settings. Fill in the email
addresses to be notified when updates are available, and click _Save
configuration_.

* Subscribe to the security announcements email list. To subscribe, log in to
_Drupal.org_, go to your user profile page and subscribe to the security
newsletter on the _Edit_ > _My newsletters_ tab.

* Follow @drupalsecurity on Twitter.

* Subscribe to RSS feeds for
http://drupal.org/security/rss.xml[core security updates],
http://drupal.org/security/contrib/rss.xml[contributed project updates] and
http://drupal.org/security/psa/rss.xml[public service announcements].
