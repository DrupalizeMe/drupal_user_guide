=== Downloading and installing a module from Drupal.org

(((Module,downloading)))
(((Module,installing)))
(((Contributed module,downloading)))
(((Contributed module,installing)))
(((Functionality,extending)))

==== Goal

Drupal has many contributed modules that help you extend the functionality of your website. Download and install the contributed module _Admin toolbar_ which allows you to easily browse through the administration section of the website.

==== Prerequisite knowledge

* <<understanding-modules>>
* <<extend-module-find>>

==== Site prerequisites

Your site must have the _Update Manager_ module enabled.

==== Steps

. On the _Admin toolbar_ project page on drupal.org (https://www.drupal.org/project/admin_toolbar) scroll to the _Downloads_ section in the bottom of the page.

. Right click the _tar.gz_ download link and copy the address.

// For this screenshot got https://www.drupal.org/project/admin_toolbar and
// crop to the downloads section. Highlight the tar.gz link.
image:images/extend-module-install-admin-toolbar-do.png[_Locating the tar.gz download link of the Admin toolbar module_,width=_100%_]

[start=3]

. In the _Manage_ administrative menu, navigate to _Extend_ (admin/modules). The _Extend_ page appears.

. Click _Install new module_. The _Install new module_ page appears.

. In the field _Install from a URL_ paste the copied download link. This value could look like `https://ftp.drupal.org/files/projects/admin_toolbar-8.x-1.14.tar.gz`

// For this screenshot follow steps 1 - 3
image:images/extend-module-install-download.png[_Downloading a module using a URL_,width=_100%_]

[start=6]

. Click _Install_ to upload and unpack the new module on the server. The files are being downloaded to the _modules_ folder.

[start=7]

. Select _Enable newly added modules_ to return to the _Extend_ page.

. Locate and select the module _Admin toolbar_.

. Click _Install_ to turn on the new module.

==== Follow-on tasks

Verify that the _Admin toolbar_ module is working by browsing through the menu in the administration section.
